About

San Pablo City

San Pablo City, located in Laguna province, is often called the “City of Seven Lakes” because of its unique natural landscape. These lakes are not only scenic but also central to the city’s culture, livelihood, and tourism. Here’s a quick overview of each:

Attractions

Sampaloc Lake

Isa sa pinakasikat na lawa sa San Pablo.

Bunot Lake

Kilala sa mga fish pens at tahimik na tanawin.

Palakpakin Lake

Isang kalmadong lawa na napapalibutan ng mga puno.

Pandin Lake

Sikat sa bamboo rafting experience.

Yambo Lake

Madalas puntahan kasama ng Pandin Lake.

Calibato Lake

Hindi masyadong matao at may relaxing na ambiance.

Mohicap Lake

Tahimik na lugar na may magandang lakeside view.
