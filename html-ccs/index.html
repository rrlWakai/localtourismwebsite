About

San Pablo City

San Pablo City, located in Laguna province, is often called the “City of Seven Lakes” because of its unique natural landscape. These lakes are not only scenic but also central to the city’s culture, livelihood, and tourism. Here’s a quick overview of each:

Attractions (Seven Lakes)

Sampaloc Lake

One of the most popular lakes in San Pablo.

Bunot Lake

Known for its fish pens and peaceful view.

Palakpakin Lake

A calm lake surrounded by greenery.

Pandin Lake

Famous for bamboo rafting experiences.

Yambo Lake

Often visited together with Pandin Lake.

Calibato Lake

Less crowded, relaxing nature atmosphere.

Mohicap Lake

Quiet spot with a scenic lakeside view.
